About Me
Hi! I'm a competitive programmer who usually uses the username hyforces (I hope you can tell from the blog title), but if you want something shorter, you can call me Fia as well. My main academic hobby is competitive programming, but I also have experience with math and physics. Outside of academics, I like to read, write, draw, and play video games.
This blog is my attempt at storing away whatever random thoughts and ideas I have, as well as noting down some of my daily experiences. It'll probably have a fair variety of competitive programming problems, essays, complaints, and so on and so forth.
